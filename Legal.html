The Dallas Business School is a nonprofit membership association offering educational programs to its members.

Dallas Business School is one of tens of thousands of membership associations in the United States which offer diploma and certificate programs to their members.

Private, nonprofit, membership associations are not subject to regulation by any state department of education.

The Dallas Business School is domiciled in Washington, DC.
