Who We Are

The Dallas Business School is a private, nonprofit, membership organization and subsidiary of University Church. Our mission is to offer graduate business education programs that are accredited, accelerated, and affordable! We believe traditional American colleges and universities overcharge students and provide inferior educational experiences! In the great American tradition of innovation and progress, we offer a better alternative for adult business education at a fraction of the cost of traditional schools!
